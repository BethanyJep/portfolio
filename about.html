DevRel, Volunteering and Art

Hi, I'm Bethany!

In Developer Relations with a passion for making technology accessible to everyone.

I believe in the power of technology to transform communities and am dedicated to building bridges between developers, products, and possibilities. My journey spans from design to code, from local meetups to international conferences, always with the goal of creating inclusive tech spaces.

When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me volunteering for tech education initiatives, experimenting with new creative tools, or enjoying a cup of tea while mentoring upcoming developers.
